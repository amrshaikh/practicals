Our Mission

Our efforts are focused on these key areas to create a sustainable and healthy planet.

Water Ecosystems

Conserve and restore vital water ecosystems, from rivers to oceans.

Forest Restoration

Deal with deforestation and restore degraded forests to combat climate change.

End Desertification

Implement strategies to halt and reverse land degradation and desertification.

Mountain Ecosystems

Conserve mountainous ecosystems, which are critical for biodiversity and water supply.

Protect Habitats

Ensure the protection of all kinds of habitats, both terrestrial and aquatic.

Genetic Resources

Promote fair access to genetic resources for the benefit of the entire globe.

Eliminate Poaching

Work towards eliminating poaching and the illegal wildlife trade.
